Taylor Apps

Your Fashion Defines You

Taylor Apps is an application that helps the customers to get the tailor easily with just a few taps. With this application, we can order for tailor service without having to go to the tailor shop itself. This application provides 2 main features. The first feature is the "Customize" feature, where customers can customize their clothes according to what they want. The second feature is "Repair" feature, where customers can repair their clothes.
